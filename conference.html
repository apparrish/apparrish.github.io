Interview Tips:

  • Contact your interviewee, tell them who you are and what you’re looking for
  • "Pre-interview"
  • Brainstorm session! Write down every possible question you might have, then put them in an order that you think might work
  • Make sure all your equipment is good; have backups
  • Model the kind of conversation you want: share anecdotes and be relaxed
  • Massage the conversation to build towards a higher point of self-reflection
  • Create the situation for interesting things to happen in your tape
  • Singing, reading out loud, show you something
  • The interviewee is speaking about a topic that you recognize as valuable, let them know. Validate the knowledge they are sharing!
  • When interviewing, it is okay to detour into conversation, even if said detour doesn’t result in information immediately relevant to what you are looking for
