Audio
Working Out Loud, 2019
Designed and led Working Out Loud, an audio storytelling workshop for Brown University undergraduate students interested in producing high quality, ethical audio stories. Working Out Loud, in partnership with the CSSJ and Now Here This, aims to deconstruct certain preconceived notions about podcasting and pull back the curtain on audio storytelling. Drawing from personal and collective narratives, students learned to use audio production and storytelling as a tool for examining themes of race, freedoms, and injustices of the past and present.
